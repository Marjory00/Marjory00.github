About Us


JoIT CS locally owned small business based in Clarksburg, MD. We are in the business of providing consultation, development, installation, and access services for computer repairs, home networks, and websites. We provide onsite or in-office service coverage for the areas of Maryland, Washington D.C and Virginia.



IT Consulting

We provide IT Consulting, Web Development, Computer Repair, and IT network services to residential customers, and small businesses to develop, use and integrate IT systems.

Design IT systems and networks ensuring the right architecture and functionality. Support new technology implementation. Provide advice to end-users in new and existing IT systems. Provide assistance with technical issues in computer and IT networks. Front End Development Services.
